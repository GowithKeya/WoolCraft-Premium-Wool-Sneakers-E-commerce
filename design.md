# Wool Sneakers E-commerce - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Core Concept: "Natural Wool + Urban Functionality"
The design embodies the intersection of natural comfort and modern city life. We create a sophisticated aesthetic that speaks to environmentally conscious consumers who refuse to compromise on style or functionality.

### Color Palette: Low-Saturation Warm Tones
- **Primary**: Warm White (#FEFCF8) - Clean, pure, reminiscent of natural wool
- **Secondary**: Oat (#F5F1E8) - Soft, neutral, organic warmth
- **Accent**: Foggy Gray (#E8E5E0) - Sophisticated, urban, subtle depth
- **Text**: Charcoal (#2C2C2C) - Strong contrast, readable, modern
- **Highlight**: Sage (#A8B5A0) - Natural, muted green for interactive elements

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Canela (serif) - Elegant, sophisticated for headings
- **Body Font**: Suisse Int'l (sans-serif) - Clean, modern, highly readable
- **Accent Font**: Tiempos (serif) - For brand statements and quotes

### Visual Language
- **Texture Focus**: Emphasize the soft, tactile quality of wool through subtle textures and organic shapes
- **Minimalist Approach**: Clean layouts with generous white space to let products breathe
- **Natural Elements**: Incorporate organic curves and soft edges throughout
- **Urban Sophistication**: Balance natural warmth with modern, city-inspired design elements

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects
1. **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and element transitions
2. **ECharts.js**: Data visualization for sustainability metrics
3. **Splide.js**: Product image carousels and testimonial sliders
4. **p5.js**: Organic background patterns and wool texture simulations
5. **Matter.js**: Subtle physics-based interactions for product displays
6. **Pixi.js**: Advanced visual effects for hero sections
7. **Shader-park**: Gradient backgrounds and atmospheric effects

### Animation & Interaction
- **Scroll Animations**: Gentle reveal effects with 16-24px vertical translation
- **Hover Effects**: 3D tilt on product cards, subtle glow on buttons
- **Loading States**: Organic wool-inspired loading animations
- **Transitions**: 300ms ease-out for all interactive elements

### Header & Navigation Effects
- **Background**: Aurora gradient flow with warm white to oat transitions
- **Navigation**: Semi-transparent backdrop with subtle blur
- **Logo**: Custom typography with subtle wool texture overlay

### Product Showcase
- **Card Design**: Clean white backgrounds with soft shadows
- **Image Treatment**: High-contrast product photography with natural lighting
- **Hover States**: Gentle lift effect with expanded shadow
- **Color Swatches**: Circular selectors with actual wool textures

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Primary Background**: Consistent warm white with subtle texture overlay
- **Section Differentiation**: Gentle oat-colored geometric shapes
- **Accent Areas**: Foggy gray panels for secondary content
- **Texture Elements**: Subtle wool fiber patterns at low opacity

### Interactive Elements
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners, sage green primary, warm gray secondary
- **Forms**: Clean inputs with soft focus states
- **Filters**: Toggle switches with organic pill shapes
- **Cart**: Slide-out panel with smooth transitions

### Mobile Considerations
- **Touch Targets**: Minimum 44px for all interactive elements
- **Typography**: Scalable font sizes maintaining hierarchy
- **Spacing**: Generous padding for comfortable mobile browsing
- **Navigation**: Collapsible menu with smooth slide animations

## Brand Personality
Sophisticated yet approachable, environmentally conscious without being preachy, modern with natural warmth. The design should feel like a premium wool sweater translated into a digital experience - comfortable, reliable, and effortlessly stylish.
